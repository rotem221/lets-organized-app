Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 2026

We want Let's Organized and this website to be usable by as many people as possible, including people with disabilities. We are committed to continually improving accessibility.

What we do

  • We aim to conform to the WCAG 2.1 level AA guidelines and to Israeli Standard IS 5568 for the website.
  • The site uses semantic HTML, readable text sizes, responsive layouts that work on phones, tablets and desktops, and supports keyboard navigation and browser zoom.
  • We aim for sufficient color contrast and provide text alternatives for images.
  • The desktop app is built with native/standard UI components and respects your system light/dark appearance and font settings, and offers a light or dark theme.

Ongoing effort

Accessibility is an ongoing process. Some parts of the app or site may not yet be fully accessible, and we work to fix issues as we find them.

Feedback & contact

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, or need content in an alternative format, please let us know — we will do our best to help and to fix it. Contact us via dnp.co.il. Please describe the issue, the page or screen, and the device/assistive technology you were using.
